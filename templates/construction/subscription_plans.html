Why Missouri-First?

National platforms like Dodge Reports don't favor St. Louis. We do. Every conversion mapped here was documented from SLDC filings, city permit records, assessor databases, and STLToday archives by someone who was physically on these job sites when they were under construction.

Our coverage extends to Springfield, Columbia, Cape Girardeau, and other Missouri metro areas — with St. Louis downtown as the anchor dataset.

How the Data is Built
  • St. Louis Development Corporation (SLDC) public records
  • City of St. Louis Assessor database (assessed value before/after)
  • Building permits via Accela / city permit portal
  • Historic tax credit filings (Missouri DHSS + Federal NPS records)
  • New Markets Tax Credit CDFI Fund public disclosures
  • STLToday archives and press release / news sources
  • On-site photography archive (1990s–present) by Gregory Woodruff
