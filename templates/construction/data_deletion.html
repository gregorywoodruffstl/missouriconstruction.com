Facebook Login — User Data Deletion

Facebook Users

If you used Facebook Login on any Cloud and Secure Limited platform and want to remove all data associated with your account, follow the steps below.

What Data We Store

When you log in with Facebook, we receive and store:

  • Your Facebook name
  • Your Facebook email address
  • Your Facebook profile photo URL
  • A secure token linking your Facebook account to our platform

We do not store your Facebook password, friends list, or private messages.

How to Request Deletion

1

Email us directly

Send a deletion request to gregory.woodruff@cloudandsecurelimited.com with subject line: "Data Deletion Request — Missouri Construction"

2

Include your Facebook email

Provide the email address associated with your Facebook account so we can locate and remove your data.

3

We will confirm within 72 hours

We will delete your account record and social login token, then send you a confirmation email.

Revoke Facebook App Access

You can also immediately revoke Missouri Construction's access to your Facebook data at any time:

  1. Go to Facebook → Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find Missouri Construction in the list
  3. Click Remove

Revoking app access on Facebook does not automatically delete data we have already stored. Please also email us to request full deletion.
