## About Seeking Springfield

Seeking Springfield is the only digital platform dedicated exclusively to the 24 cities named Springfield across the United States. We deliver AI-powered city guides, U.S. Census demographic data, local events, business directories, and travel content for every Springfield — from Springfield, MO (population 170,000) to Springfield, IL (the state capital of Illinois).

We are a Microsoft Azure-hosted, Django-powered web application built and maintained by Cloud and Secure Limited, a registered Missouri technology company and certified Microsoft Partner.

- **Event discovery** — local events calendar pulling from 24 municipal sources

## Travel & Lodging Content Focus

Each Springfield city page features:

- City overview and key attractions
- Population, median income, and U.S. Census demographics
- Upcoming local events
- Nearby points of interest, state parks, and national landmarks
- **Hotel and accommodation search** (powered by affiliate partners including Booking.com)
- Weather integration
- Local sports teams and schedules

Lodging is a natural and high-intent need for our audience — users researching a Springfield they plan to visit are actively in the booking consideration phase.
